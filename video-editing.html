Storytelling Through Video

I'm a video editor with a strong foundation in storytelling; especially when it comes to setting the tone, evoking emotion through music, and crafting compelling intros. My path began on YouTube, where I started a channel called JohnWriting to document my journey of becoming a novelist. But after 18 months I realized I was more passionate about storytelling through video than writing. That realization set me on the path to become a freelance editor. And now I'm seeking to put my skills to use in service of something greater than myself.

Uncovering a Forgotten Insight of St. Therese

A Culmination of Years of Learning

This was my most ambitious video yet: a full video essay blending narrative, sound design, and motion graphics to explore an often-overlooked perspective of St. Therese. Building on my first deep dive into After Effects, I pushed the visuals even further to create a richer experience.

I handled everything from research to final cut, with generous feedback from friends and family along the way. It was such a joy to learn more from St. Therese, while helping others see this new side of her spirituality!

Mini Documentary - Directed and Produced

This was a HUGE learning experience! As the director and producer, I took on the responsibility of figuring out the entire creative vision for the project. It was filmed and edited over the course of a year and 99% of it was filmed on a smartphone (some of the biggest lessons I learned were the importance of being familiar with what you will be filming beforehand and that with only one camera the focus needs to be on capturing the scene itself as it happens, not trying to get artsy shots)
